3D Modeling Project

The process of 3D design has always triggered my curiosity. In my undergrad experience, I had the opportunity to take a 3D Modeling course that broadened my experience. In this class, we were prompted to find something in our room to model. Since I had a particular interest in color and textures, I decided to make my model of an eyeshadow palette that I had around. Below is the documentation of the whole process as well as final renderings in different environments. It was very gratifying to see the palette come to life after multiple sketches and iterations.
