Hello

My name is Carolyn. My passion for visual design began with my love for hand lettering. I enjoy the challenge of creating solutions for what needs to be communicated and representing it in the most creative, engaging way. Words are powerful and visual communication holds great importance in our society. From these interests, I decided to pursue web development as it allows me to work with clients to build a user experience that will grow their brand and mission. I thrive in creative environments that are in a consistent state of growth. As a web designer, I am very proactive in finding creative solutions and have learned to be adaptable to client feedback.

When I'm not working...

In my free time, I enjoy being creative in the kitchen with new recipes. The experience of going into a Trader Joe's can feel like Disney World for me while imagining all the new food to try. My second favorite thing to do is exploring new cities - whether internationally around Europe or in my home state of North Carolina. Every city has its own personality and draw which I enjoy uncovering.
