About

Hello, my name is Carolyn. My passion for visual design began with my love for hand lettering. I enjoy the challenge of creating solutions for what needs to be communicated and representing it in the most creative, engaging way. Words are powerful and visual communication holds great importance in our society. From these interests, I decided to pursue web development as it allows me to work with clients to design a user experience that will grow their brand and mission. I thrive in creative environments that are in a consistent state of growth.
