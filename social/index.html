Social Coffee Rebranding

For this project in a Graphic Design course, I was assigned to select a local business in Wilmington and go through a substantial rebranding process. Social Coffee Supply & Co is a craft coffee shop with the goal of giving back locally and globally to nonprofits. The following process book shows the progression of my work throughout the semester. The final products included a new logo, stationery set, mobile app icon, brochure, and custom ad design.
