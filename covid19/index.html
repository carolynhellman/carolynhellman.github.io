Covid-19 Animated Explainer Video

For this animated explainer video, I researched how Covid-19 has affected romantic relationships. During my research, I found a wide variety of tips for maintaining a healthy relationship while being in lockdown together 24/7 or being forced into a long-distance relationship to stay safe. I began by using Illustrator and Photoshop to create all of the custom illustrations and animated everything in After Effects.
